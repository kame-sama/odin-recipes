Cinnamon Bread

This easy cinnamon bread recipe is full of cozy flavor and features a crumbly cinnamon topping.

Ingredients

  • For the bread: all-purpose flour, white sugar, buttermilk, eggs, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, baking powder, ground cinnamon, salt, and baking soda
  • For the topping: white sugar, butter or margarine, and ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Make the batter and pour it into a prepared loaf pan.
  2. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the batter.
  3. Use a paring knife to swirl batter for a marbled effect.
  4. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
